Token utility

ENK is the coordination, access, governance, and ecosystem utility asset of the EnteleKRON network across AI, blockchain, identity, energy, and infrastructure modules. It is not a simple payment token.

Utility pillars

  • Governance and treasury signaling across ecosystem modules
  • Infrastructure and module access (validators, premium services)
  • TVK ID and sovereign attestation utility flows
  • SOVRA-based AI services, diagnostics, and automation
  • Cross-ecosystem service coordination as modules go live

Transparency

EnteleKRON uses a controlled treasury transparency model. Official ecosystem treasury, vesting, and governance addresses are published through the EnteleKRON Transparency Center. Always verify addresses through entelekron.io, entelekron.app, and official announcements.
