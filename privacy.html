Privacy Policy

How EnteleSCAN processes personal data.

Last updated: 2026-07-07 · Operated by T.V.K. Labs & Technologies LTD

EnteleSCAN (operated by T.V.K. Labs & Technologies LTD, "TVK Labs") processes personal data in connection with platform usage, contact requests, validator applications, ecosystem collaboration, and communications related to the ENTELΞKRON observatory layer.

Data we may process

  • Contact details (name, email address) when you reach out via email or contact forms
  • Wallet addresses submitted through the universal search or explorer features (processed transiently for lookup, not stored as personal profiles)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage analytics
  • Validator application data: hardware specifications, jurisdiction, and operational experience where submitted

How we use data

Data is used to operate the EnteleSCAN platform, respond to inquiries, improve observatory features, support ecosystem integrations, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal data.

Third-party services

EnteleSCAN uses Etherscan API for on-chain data retrieval. Google Translate may process page content for multilingual display. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data retention

Contact and application data is retained as long as necessary for the purpose collected and in accordance with legal and regulatory requirements. Transient search queries are not persisted on EnteleSCAN servers.

Your rights

You may exercise applicable rights under GDPR, KVKK, and other regulations by contacting legal@tvk.group. For investor-related data processed through EnteleKRON, see the EnteleKRON Privacy Policy.
