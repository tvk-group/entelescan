How It Works

Observe, analyze, explain, and verify.

EnteleSCAN follows a four-layer intelligence flow for turning raw network data into actionable insight.

Step 01

Ingest

Collect chain data, validator metrics, wallet events, bridge messages, governance records, and proof anchors.

Step 02

Index

Organize raw network information into searchable records, timelines, dashboards, and explorer views.

Step 03

Analyze

Apply AI diagnostics, anomaly detection, validator scoring, risk patterns, and ecosystem health logic.

Step 04

Explain

Provide human-readable insights, summaries, alerts, proof links, and operational recommendations.
